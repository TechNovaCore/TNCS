Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Quick answers to questions we get all the time.

Do you provide on-site service in Mumbai?

Yes! We provide on-site service across Mumbai — Goregaon, Malad, Andheri, Borivali, Kandivali and nearby areas. We also offer remote support for issues that can be resolved online.

What brands of computers do you repair?

We service all major brands — Dell, HP, Lenovo, Asus, Acer, Samsung, and more. We also handle industrial computers, POS terminals, Zebra/Honeywell barcode printers, and Canon/Epson printers.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency call?

For emergency issues, we typically respond within 1–2 hours. Remote support can often begin within minutes of your call. Sunday and after-hours emergency support is available for critical business systems.

Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?

Yes! We offer flexible AMC packages for businesses of all sizes. AMC covers regular maintenance visits, priority support, discounted repair rates, and free remote assistance. Contact us for a custom quote.

Can you install and configure CCTV systems for my office?

Absolutely. We supply and install Hikvision, Dahua and other CCTV systems including IP cameras, DVR/NVR setup, cabling, and remote viewing configuration via your phone or computer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), NEFT/IMPS, and bank transfers. Invoices with GST are provided for all business transactions.
